Title: Color Metamorphosis

Tools: Sharpie and acrylic paints on bristol

For this project, I morphed two different things together, which are a tree and a face. The tree makes out some features of a face through its branches. The purpose of this assignment was to incorporate all the colors throughout the color wheel. So, throughout the drawing, it goes through the color scale forward and backwards, both ending and starting with yellow.

Title: Chungha Digital Drawing

Tools: Procreate

This is the first drawing that I have ever done on Procreate, as I am fairly new to the realm of digital drawing. It was interesting to play around with the different pressure points of the Apple Pencil and how it affected the different brushes that I used. I worked on this project slowly across several weeks, as it seemed quite intimating to tackle as I had never used such a program before. I am definitely excited to explore more of the options that Procreate has to offer.

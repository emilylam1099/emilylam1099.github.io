Title: Minimal Letterforms

Tools: Illustrator

In this project, I chose a series of three different groupings of character in order to create a visual harmony between all of their parts. However, the idea was to use their most distinctive qualities in order to create the composition. By exploring the minimalism in the the letterforms, it revealed much about the principles of Gestalt and how each piece of the composition worked to form a cohesive whole.
